007: Grand Bazaar, Thomas Newman(Skyfall Theme)

Skyfall is the soundtrack album to the 23rd James Bond film of the same name. Released by Sony Classical on 29 October 2012 in the United Kingdom the music was composed by Thomas Newman.

Fallout, Lorne Balfe(Mission Impossible Theme)

Fallout is the main theme tune of the series Mission Impossible: Fallout (2018). It is based on "Theme from Mission: Impossible", written and composed by Argentine composer Lalo Schifrin

Escape Through Paris, Lorne Balfe(Music from Mission Impossible)

One of the most popular pieces from Mission Impossible: Fallout (2018), that fits perfect with Ethan Hunt attempt to escape alive from his deadly mission.

Minority Report, John Williams(Minority Report Theme)

The main theme of Minority Report is the philosophical debate of free will vs. determinism. One of the main questions is whether the future is set or whether free will can alter the future. Do we possess free will or are we predestined to our fate?.
